project overview

our team of 4 designers created a
marketplace for events and spaces

my contributions included

   original interface design
   brand design
   presentation design

100%  of users enjoyed the experience

100%  said it melded seamlessly with the airbnb experience

personas

aaron

aaron's seeking to share a space on airbnb

he's a young millenial who enjoys helping others, actively seeking new connections, and creating meaningful experiences

beth

beth's seeking an open space to host her event

she is a responsible, confident professional who follows a strict schedule

problem statement

event hosts are seeking spaces to host their events

how might we connect available spaces with events seamlessly through the airbnb platform?

solution

we wireframed a solution that allows events and spaces to exist independently on the platform - users can find one another via a central events hub

tasks

introducing "ace events" branding

users are tasked with either posting a space to be found (aaron's flow), or posting an event and finding a space suitable for that event (beth's flow)

iteration 2

in our second iteration, we introduced a "mode" feature for our events marketplace, where relevant paths (space (host) or event (guest)) could be selected from a similar events page. this was an unfamiliar experience for users

for example

  landing page CTA "mode" was unclear for the task flow, distracting
  landing page search bar suggestions were confusing
